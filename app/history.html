Why is important to know the signal history?

Signal history is the instrument that allows the trader to monitor the signal’s behavior in the past. Statistics are collected basing on 20 previous signals. To monitor the movement of a signal, set the following filters: algorithm, expiration date, assets.

It is important to know the signal history instead of relying on comments concerning the binary options. When the market volatility is high, absolutely any strategy or forecast based on technical analysis can be negative.

Having analyzed the signals history and the economic calendar, the trader will have a complete understanding of the market situation, which will help him to avoid the losses.
